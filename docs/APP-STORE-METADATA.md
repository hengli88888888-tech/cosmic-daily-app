## Description

```text
Oraya Insight is a self-reflection app inspired by traditional timing-based divination.

Ask one clear question about work, relationships, school, money, timing, or personal decisions, and receive a focused reading for the moment.

Each reading starts with a clear short answer, then gives practical context and next steps you can reflect on. You can save readings, continue a thread with follow-up details, add optional birth information for more personalized insight readings, and record outcome feedback when a reading becomes eligible.

Oraya Insight is for entertainment and self-reflection only. It does not provide medical, legal, financial, emergency, or other professional advice, and it does not guarantee outcomes.
```
